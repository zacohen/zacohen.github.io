Humidity

From the dataset of over 500 cities from the OpenWeatherMap API, this plot represents the correlation between city latitude and humidity. One can notice that the majority of the cities have a humidity between 60% & 100% with a majority of those between -20 and 40 Latitude. However, considering the overall high dispersion, i would consider this a minor correlation.
